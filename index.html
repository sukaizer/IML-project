Welcome to FaceMusic

This is an app using Marcelle made to help you understand live predictions of ML models for image recognition

1 - Activate your webcam and take a few snapshots for a particular face

2 - Setup a new label and do it again for a new facial expression

3 - Now you can go to the Training tab and press the training button

4 - Go to the Inspect Prediction tab and activate your webcam

Some music will play whether it's from class 'a' or 'b'

5 - You're good to go ! Now press this button and start !
